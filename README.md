# LPU Online

## About LPU Online

LPU Online is the digital education platform of Lovely Professional University (LPU), one of India's leading universities. It provides flexible and accessible online degree programs for students and working professionals worldwide.

The platform combines academic excellence, industry-relevant curriculum, and modern technology to deliver an engaging online learning experience.

- Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
- Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
- Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
- Bachelor of Arts (BA)
